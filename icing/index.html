Icing on the Cake Bakery

Icing On The Cake Bakery is owned by Donna Gummere. Baking has been my passion since I was a young girl.

I attended culinary school specializing in cakes and pastries. I have continued to develop my skills specializing in wedding cakes, specialty cakes, and French pastries.

My designs are unique to each customer and their vision of the perfect cake. My flavors of cake and cheesecakes are amazing and made from the freshest and finest ingredients possible.

I would love to make a special cake or pastry for your special occasion. Visit the contact page to get info on how to contact me.
