About

Well, My name is Jason Storey and I design websites. Shocking isn't it?

But it's not just a hobby, oh no, it has turned into a full fledged obsession that I intend to spend the rest of my life fulfilling. Which brings us here, to my website.

I am currently a freelance web designer living and learning in Southern California. I spend most of my free time building web pages or learning everything I can to better by design skills and advance myself in the web design industry. Fluent in HTML and CSS, I pride myself on designing web sites using standards-oriented, valid, and reusable code. "Cleanliness is next to Godliness", as the saying goes.

I am currently working towards a BS in Computer Science as well as learning and exploring javascript, PHP, and MySQL with hopes of adding these to my "fluent" category very soon.

Expertise in Adobe CS6 including: Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and Flash.
