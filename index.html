About Me

My name is Jason Storey and I am a Front-End Developer living in Sunny Orange County, California. Front-End Development is not my job; it is my passion. Whether I'm just styling up a simple form or taking a PSD and coding an entire site, I love the web and everything that comes with it. Learning is a neccesity for me. Being completely self taught I always had more to learn, and I never wanted to stop. Even to this day you can usually find me spending free time researching new techniques or learning new frameworks.

I try to increase my skills in every area that I work in but recently I have been really getting into mobile/responsive development. Using jQuery Mobile and Zurb's Foundation 4, I have built mobile sites for mutiple clients in the Mortgage Industry as well as the site you are on right now; mine.

My knowledge of HTML5/CSS3, Javascript, and jQuery Mobile are the cornerstones of my abilites. Add to this my continuing self education of PHP/MySQL and Javascript libraries like Node.js, and Backbone.js and you can see that I am always busy trying to learn as much of what makes the web work as possible.
