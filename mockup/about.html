My parents crossed the great Atlantic. Behind them was a world of heritage and pride. Ahead of them was an untold future. I was only a little girl when I was first exposed to flowers. I wasn’t exposed the way other children tended to learn about flowers, but rather in a unique way. Although I am first generation American, I cant hide that I am a daughter of Holland, a beautiful land that has flowers running through its veins. With my daughter and son, we are both third and fourth generation florists. My Grandfather, Uncles, Cousins, and “Greats” alike worked in the floral business. We know our flowers. Inga's Blooms is a company of inspiration and challenge and we are based on the belief that our customers needs are of the utmost importance. We are committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals. My goal, as a daughter, a mother, an artist, and a friend, is to share with you my love of flowers and my love of design. Inga’s Blooms promises to bring the earth to your heart on your very special day. We would welcome the opportunity to earn your trust and deliver you the best service in the floral industry. We are committed to fair prices. We deliver to Central and South Orange County!
