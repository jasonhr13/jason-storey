Our Mission

The Law Firm of Smock and Etling strives to provide each and every client with the best possible criminal defense, personal injury advocacy, or counsel in the area of family law.

The Law Firm of Smock and Etling has handled cases throughout Indiana in both State and Federal Court. The Firm regularly serves the citizens of Vigo, Sullivan, Clay, Greene, Knox, Parke, Vermillion and Putnam Counties. The Firm's practice concentrates primarily on Criminal Defense of all felonies and misdemeanors, D.W.I., Drunk Driving, Personal Injury, and Family Law.
